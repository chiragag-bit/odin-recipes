Burger Recipe

Description:

This is great recipe for most tempting burger making that tastes delicious to your taste buds

Ingredients:

  • Carrot
  • Cauliflower
  • Beans
  • Sweet corn
  • Peas
  • Salt
  • Potato
  • Cumin powder
  • Garam Masala
  • Turmeric
  • Kashmiri red chilli powder
  • Chaat masala
  • ocriander,finely chopped
  • bread crumbs
  • Buns

Steps:

  1. Steam all vegetabales for 10 min
  2. Transfer steamed vegetables on medium flame
  3. Add 3 boiled and ashed potato
  4. add cuminpowder, garam masala,turmeric, chilli powder,chaat masala,salt
  5. Add coriander and mix well.
  6. Add 1/4 cup breaad crumbs
  7. Prepare slurry by making 2 tbsp cornflour, 1/4 cup maida, and 1/2 cup water in small bowl.
  8. Coat with breadcrumbs uniformly.
  9. Shallow fry in hot oil keeping the flame on medium.
  10. Fry on both sides,until it turns golden brown,ddrain off and keep aside.
