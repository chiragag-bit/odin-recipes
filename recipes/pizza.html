Pizza Recipe

Description:

This is the most effective and tasty recipe of making most Tangy and cheesiest pizza for you!!!

Ingredients:

  • Warm water
  • Granulated sugar
  • Instant yeast
  • All-purpose flour
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt
  • Cornmeal

Steps:

  1. Prepare and rise the dough
  2. Preheat the oven and prep
  3. Shape and top pizza
  4. Bake and serve
